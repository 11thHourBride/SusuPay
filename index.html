‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process contributions?

Contributions are typically reviewed within 24-48 hours. You'll receive a notification once your contribution is approved.

Why is my withdrawal pending?

Withdrawals require manual approval for security reasons. Processing time is usually 24-72 hours depending on the payment method.

How do I complete KYC verification?

Go to KYC Verification in the menu, accept the terms, upload your ID documents (front and back), and take a live selfie. Our team will review within 2-5 business days.

Can I change my withdrawal password?

Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Change Withdrawal Password. You'll need to enter your current account password for security verification.

What payment methods are supported?

We support Mobile Money (MTN, Telecel, AirtelTigo), Cryptocurrency (USDT, BTC, ETH, USDC), and Bank Transfers. You can manage your payment methods in the Payment Methods section.
